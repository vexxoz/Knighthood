Welcome to Garethdale!

Garethdale is the renown hub of shops and bartery. Merchants travel great distances to make it to this legendary castle and city. Walking down the streets one can see local merchants trading their load. Farmers buying new tools. Blacksmiths hammering away on their anvils creating a new device for combat. The smell of the air is rich, the people are easy going and friendly. Garethdale is truely a economic and culture capital of the Valteri Empire!

Welcome to Rotterhye!

Rotterhye is a military fort. With its high towers and overwhelming number of trops, its a fortress not to be triffled with. Here lies warriors and knights seeking to train with the best, at a high cost. The local people are prideful and seek independece from Valteri. Any king loyalist would tell you to stay away from this place.

Welcome to Shireston!

Shireston is the Capital of the Valteri Empire and is home to King Walter. The town thrives and is the most populated in all of the Empire. Shireston is the second largest economic powerhouse behind Garethdale. With the current tension between Rotterhye and the Empire guard count is doubled.

Welcome to Oldfernan!

Oldfernan is a quiet community. Full of lush parks and fountains. Not much happens and the locals like it that way. They are loyal to the king but have no prejudice against the rebels. You have less of a chance of finding a duel here.
